Which cup is used for under-eye treatment?

The smallest cup (approximately 1.5cm diameter) is used for the under-eye area with barely a squeeze of suction. This gently moves lymphatic fluid, reducing puffiness and dark circles. Never apply more than the lightest suction in the orbital area.

How do I clean and store the facial cups?

Wash with gentle soap and warm water after each use, rinse thoroughly, and air dry completely. Store in included case away from direct sunlight. Silicon degrades slowly under UV exposure.

Can these cups be used on the scalp for hair loss?
