Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  Detoxy Hijama, Pollachi, Tamil Nadu

Detoxy Hijama ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account, we collect:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Delivery address
  • Order history and payment method (not card details)
  • Anonymous website usage data (page views, device type)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process, fulfil, and ship your orders
  • To send order confirmations, invoices, and delivery updates
  • To respond to your customer service enquiries
  • To send occasional promotional emails (you can unsubscribe at any time)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share only the minimum necessary information with:

  • Shipping partners (name, phone, address) to deliver your order
  • Razorpay (payment processor) for secure transaction processing

Data Security

All data is stored securely. Payment transactions are encrypted using SSL/TLS. We do not store credit or debit card numbers. Our systems follow standard security best practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Email us at detoxyhijama@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember your cart and login session. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect cart functionality.
