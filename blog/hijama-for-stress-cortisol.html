Suction Mechanics for Hijama For Stress Cortisol: Technical Guide

The therapeutic suction level for stress cortisol conditions falls in the range of approximately 200–400 millibars negative pressure for most adult patients. The incision phase of wet hijama for stress cortisol requires precise technique to achieve the therapeutic blood extraction while minimising unnecessary tissue trauma.
