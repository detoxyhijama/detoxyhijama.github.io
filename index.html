Frequently Asked Questions

What is hijama and how does it work?

Hijama is the ancient practice of therapeutic cupping — placing suction cups on the skin to create negative pressure that draws blood and toxins to the surface, decompresses muscles, stimulates blood flow, and activates the body's natural healing mechanisms.

Which hijama cup size should I use?

Large cups: back, thighs, chest. Medium: shoulders, knees, calves. Small: face, hands, joints. Curved cups: shoulders, elbows, uneven surfaces. Our Premium Silicone Set includes all sizes in one box.
