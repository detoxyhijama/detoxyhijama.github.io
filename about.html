Who We Are

Born from a Practitioner's Need

Detoxy Hijama was founded in Pollachi, Tamil Nadu by hijama practitioners who were frustrated with the lack of reliable, affordable cupping equipment available in India. Most products were imported with high markups, poor quality control, or simply unavailable.

We started manufacturing our own cups, kits, and consumables — factory direct, at honest prices. Today we serve thousands of independent practitioners, clinics, hospitals, and training institutes across every state in India.

Every product we make is tested against strict quality standards before it reaches your hands. Our promise: genuine materials, fair pricing, and service you can count on.
