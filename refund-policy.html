Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  Detoxy Hijama, Pollachi, Tamil Nadu
✅ We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy on all non-consumable products.

Eligibility for Returns

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for products that are: unused and in original packaging, defective or damaged on arrival, or significantly not as described on our website.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons:

  • Opened consumables: surgical blades, latex gloves, surgical cotton
  • Products with signs of use or without original packaging after 7 days
  • Items damaged due to misuse, improper storage, or customer negligence

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at detoxyhijama@gmail.com or call +91 95665 96077
  2. Include your Order ID and clear photos of the product and packaging
  3. Our team will approve the return within 24 hours and arrange pickup if applicable
  4. For defective items, return shipping is fully reimbursed

Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the return
  • Bank transfer (NEFT) takes 2–3 business days
  • COD orders are refunded via bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS)

Exchanges

We accept product exchanges within 7 days. If exchanging for a higher-priced product, you pay the difference. Contact us to arrange.
