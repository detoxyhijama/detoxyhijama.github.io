Indian Standard Hijama Cup — Affordable Domestic-Grade Silicone Cupping

The Detoxy Hijama Indian Standard Cup is manufactured domestically in India, offering practitioners an economical and dependable silicone cupping option. Available in 6 standard sizes suitable for all major hijama points on the body.

How does this differ from the Premium China Made Cups?

The Indian Standard Cup is our entry-level clinic cup — excellent for high-volume practices that need reliable, cost-effective cups. The Premium China Made Cups offer a thicker wall, firmer suction, and include Curve Cup variants for joints. Both are silicone and safe for wet and dry cupping.

Can these be used for wet hijama?

Yes. These cups are suitable for both wet (with incision) and dry cupping. Clean between patients by boiling in water or wiping with 70% isopropyl alcohol.
