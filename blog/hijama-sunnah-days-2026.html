The Prophetic Sunnah Days for Hijama

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ recommended cupping on the 17th, 19th, and 21st of the Islamic lunar month. These are sometimes extended to the 17th–21st of any Hijri month. Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays are also considered preferred days by many scholars.

Note: Ramadan (Sha'ban end / Ramadan 1447: ~Feb–Mar 2026) is generally avoided for wet hijama due to fasting. Dry cupping is permissible during Ramadan according to many scholars.

Days to Avoid

Scholars generally advise avoiding hijama on Wednesdays, Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays, as well as on the 2nd, 3rd, and last day of the Hijri month. These are traditional guidelines — consult a qualified Islamic scholar for religious rulings.
