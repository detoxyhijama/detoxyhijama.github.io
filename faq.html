🩺 About Hijama
What is hijama cupping therapy?
Hijama is an ancient form of alternative medicine in which suction cups are placed on the skin to draw out stagnant blood, toxins, and cellular waste. It is a prophetic Sunnah practice with documented benefits for pain relief, blood circulation, detoxification, and immune function. Our blog has detailed articles on every aspect of hijama therapy.
What is the difference between wet and dry cupping?
Dry cupping involves placing cups on the skin using suction only — no incisions. It is great for muscle relaxation, lymphatic drainage, and pain relief. Wet hijama (Al-Hijama) involves making small superficial incisions after dry cupping to draw out a small amount of blood. It is the traditional Sunnah method. Read our detailed blog posts for more guidance.
Which kit is recommended for beginners?
For beginners we recommend the Magnetic Vacuum Cupping Kit for easy suction control, or the Premium Silicone Cup set for flexibility. Both come with instructions. You will also need surgical blades, latex gloves, and cotton for a complete setup.
