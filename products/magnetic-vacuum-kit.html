The Magnetic Lock Advantage

The magnetic connector system is the defining feature that separates this kit from standard vacuum cup sets. A rare-earth magnet in the pump connector aligns precisely with the metal ring in the cup valve, creating an airtight, wobble-free connection that maintains full suction during every pump stroke. Standard plastic connectors rely on friction fit and frequently slip, losing suction and requiring repositioning during treatment. The magnetic system eliminates this problem entirely — each stroke builds precisely, and removal is a clean single twist without disturbing the cup position.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the magnetic lock system?

A rare-earth magnet in the pump gun connector locks precisely onto a metal ring in each cup valve, creating an airtight connection with no slipping during pumping. Release is a clean single-twist — never disturbing cup position during treatment.

How do I adjust suction strength?

Each pump stroke adds suction. 2-3 strokes = light suction; 4-6 strokes = medium therapeutic suction; 7+ strokes = deep suction for thick muscle groups. The release valve on the cup top provides instant pressure relief.

Is this kit suitable for beginners?

This is one of the best beginner kits because the pump gun provides controlled, measurable suction unlike fire or squeeze methods. The 4-size range teaches appropriate cup selection for different body areas.

What is included in the carry case?

Can I buy replacement cups separately?

Yes — the cups are standard valve-top cups compatible with any suction gun. Individual replacement cups are available. The gun is designed for thousands of uses with proper care.
